Frequently Asked Questions

Get Answers to Your Questions

Find answers to commonly asked questions about Teacher Linked Management System (TLMS).

  • What is TLMS and how does it work?

    TLMS, or Teacher Linked Management System, is an innovative online platform that simplifies the process of teacher relocations. TLMS allows educators to connect with each other and explore opportunities to exchange work locations, making the relocation process seamless and efficient.

  • How can I create a profile on TLMS?

    To create a profile on TLMS, you can visit our website and click on the "Register" button. Follow the registration process to set up your profile by providing information about your teaching experience, specialties, and desired relocation destinations.

  • How can TLMS help me in my teaching career?

    TLMS offers educators the opportunity to explore new teaching environments and connect with colleagues who are looking to exchange work locations. This can expand your teaching horizons, create new opportunities, and foster a sense of community among educators.

  • Is TLMS secure for exchanging sensitive documents?

    Yes, TLMS provides a secure environment for teachers to exchange necessary documents and details for a smooth transition during relocations. We prioritize the security and privacy of our users' information.

  • How can I stay updated on potential swap opportunities?

    TLMS sends real-time notifications when compatible swap opportunities arise. This ensures that you are always informed and never miss out on promising matches.

  • How can I get in touch with TLMS support?

    If you need assistance or have questions, you can contact TLMS support through our website or by emailing Abrahamkijazi01@gmail.com. Our dedicated support team is here to help you.
